Purpose

The Yenagoa Nigeria Stake Returned Missionaries Association(RMA), is a separate body and entity but united for the same mission and aim of The Church Of Jesus Christ Of Latter-Day Saints, in pushing forward the course of the gathering of Israel in this dispensation.

Audience

Are you seeking to grow intecllectually, socially, physically and spiritually, in Favour with God and Man, The Yenagoa Nigeria Stake Returned Missionaries Association is here to uplift, support and encourage kindreds, tongues and people, in their course towards spiritual and temporal sefl-reliance, through the restored gospel. Encouraging and assisting young minds to be converted to the savior, serving as a full time missionary as every member of this association did, as an association, we are united in showing compassion as the savior did by rendering services to those in need, charitable donations, grants, and scholarships when possible.

Home page

Yenagoa, Nigeria Stake Returned Missionaries Association Mission: Strengthening, Serving, and Sharing the Light of the Restored Gospel Introduction Welcome to the Yenagoa, Nigeria Stake Returned Missionaries Association! Our mission is to unite and empower returned full-time missionaries who have served in various corners of the world and bring them together to make a positive impact on our local community and the missionary work within our stake. We are a group of passionate individuals who are committed to strengthening one another, helping young men and women prepare for their missions, and improving the state of missionary work in the Yenagoa Nigeria Stake. Additionally, we are dedicated to giving back through charitable donations and community service projects. Join us as we continue to share the light of the gospel and serve others. Our Mission The Yenagoa, Nigeria Stake Returned Missionaries Association is driven by the principle of service. We aim to: 1. Strengthen the bonds among returned missionaries. 2. Assist and mentor young men and women in their journey to serve missions. 3. Enhance the state of missionary work within our stake. 4. Engage in charitable donations and community service to uplift our local community. Strengthening One Another As returned missionaries, we understand the unique challenges and blessings that come with serving a mission. Through regular gatherings, events, and social activities, we provide a support network that helps returning missionaries transition smoothly back into daily life. We share experiences, provide emotional support, and offer guidance as our members navigate the challenges of post-mission life. Together, we foster an environment of understanding and encouragement, building lasting friendships and strengthening the spiritual and emotional well-being of each member. Preparing Future Missionaries Our association is committed to assisting young men and women in the Yenagoa Nigeria Stake who are preparing to serve missions. We offer mentorship, guidance, and workshops to help them navigate the application process, develop vital missionary skills, and deepen their understanding of the gospel. By sharing our own experiences, we strive to inspire and prepare the next generation of missionaries, ensuring they embark on their service with confidence and a strong foundation in the teachings of Jesus Christ. Improving Missionary Work To enhance the state of missionary work within the Yenagoa Nigeria Stake, our association collaborates with local leaders and missionaries to identify and address specific needs. We organize outreach programs, assist with teaching and fellowshipping investigators, and engage in missionary activities that help bring the light of the gospel to our community. By working hand in hand with the full-time missionaries, we create a synergy that strengthens the Church and bolsters missionary efforts. Charitable Donations and Service Projects One of the core values of our association is the spirit of giving back. We actively engage in charitable donations and service projects that benefit the local community and those in need. We participate in various initiatives, such as food drives, clothing donations, and volunteer work. Through our collective efforts, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of others and reflect the love of Christ in our actions. Our commitment to service is an essential part of our mission, and we encourage all members to join in these efforts. Get Involved Are you a returned missionary from the Yenagoa, Nigeria Stake, or are you interested in supporting our mission? We invite you to join our association and be part of a community dedicated to strengthening, serving, and sharing the light of the gospel. Together, we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of returned missionaries, future missionaries, and our local community. Join us today and be a part of this inspiring journey. Conclusion The Yenagoa, Nigeria Stake Returned Missionaries Association is a testament to the enduring bonds created through missionary service. As we unite to strengthen one another, prepare future missionaries, improve missionary work, and give back to our community, we exemplify the principles of love and service taught by Jesus Christ. Together, we continue to shine the light of the Restored gospel in Yenagoa, Nigeria Stake.
